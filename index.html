About Prairie

short legs, tall barks!

Hatched from a unicorn egg, Prairie is a stealthy little rascal who steals pillows, balls, and hearts. She made her big debut into this Earth in 2012, or was it 2014? 2013!? Who knows! She is a proud member of the elusive Oogle Boogle species.

After spending her first few years in Garden City, Kansas, Prairie was snatched from the throes of death by an overnight truck driver to live in foster care in Manhattan, Kansas. There, she was given the name Prairie for her propensity to stand on her hind legs and beg for treats. In 2015 she moved to Columbia, Missouri where she rescued her parents and mastered throwing the ball as a coping mechanism for her anxiety. No, really! She can throw!

Ever-seeking change and a new adventure, Prairie packed her ball, space squirrel, and donut squeaker (the essentials) and headed north to Calgary, Alberta. She currently spends her days trolling the neighbourhood magpies and bragging to her American friends about the healthcare and giant jackrabbits. Prairie's interests include long walks, licking the floor incessantly, spelunking under the bed, stealing bones from her older sister, Pippa, and listening to Mitski. She is seeking employment as a treat tester.
